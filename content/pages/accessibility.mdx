At Unidemy, we are committed to ensuring accessibility for all users, regardless of ability. We strive to continuously improve the accessibility of our website [unidemy.tech](https://unidemy.tech) to provide an inclusive experience.

## Accessibility Features

Our efforts to enhance accessibility include:

- **Keyboard Navigation**: Our website is navigable using keyboard controls for users who may not use a mouse.
- **Alt Text**: We strive to provide descriptive alt text for images to aid users using screen readers.
- **Color Contrast**: We maintain sufficient color contrast to assist users with visual impairments.
- **Readable Text**: Content is presented in a clear and readable manner.

## Ongoing Improvements

We are committed to ongoing efforts to improve accessibility. We regularly review our website to identify areas for enhancement and take necessary steps to address them.

## Feedback

Your feedback is valuable to us. If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at support@unidemy.tech.

## Standards Compliance

We aim to comply with WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) to ensure our website is accessible to the widest possible audience.

## Contact Us

For any accessibility-related inquiries or concerns, please contact us at support@unidemy.tech.
