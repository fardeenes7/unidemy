This Privacy Policy describes how Unidemy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects the information we gather through our website [unidemy.tech](https://unidemy.tech) ("Site").

## Information Collection and Use

We collect and use certain personal information when users log in, such as:

- Name
- Email Address (obtained through social login providers)

### Purpose of Data Collection

The collected information is used to:

- Create and maintain user profiles
- Personalize user experiences
- Communicate with users regarding services, updates, or inquiries
- Analyze and improve our services

### Data Usage and Sharing

We do not share any personal data collected with third parties.

## Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies for analytical purposes. This includes services like:

- Vercel Analytics
- Facebook Pixel
- Google Analytics

These tools help us understand user behavior, preferences, and optimize our website.

## Security Measures

We employ robust security measures to ensure the safe storage and protection of user information in remote databases.

- Encryption techniques are used to safeguard data transmission.
- Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

## User Rights

### Access, Correction, and Deletion

Users can request access to their personal data, rectification of inaccuracies, or deletion of their information by contacting us at support@unidemy.tech.

Please note:

- Name and email collected via social login cannot be edited directly on our platform.
- Account deletion options will soon be available in the user profile.

## Payment Information

We do not store users' personal financial information (such as card details). We retain transaction history solely for accounting and record-keeping purposes.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date mentioned and may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and users are encouraged to review this policy regularly for updates.

## Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please contact us at support@unidemy.tech.
